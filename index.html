Théo Borém Fabris

I'm a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen affiliated to BARC. I'm glad to have Amir Yehudayoff and Srikanth Srinivasan as my PhD advisors. I did my undergrad at the University of São Paulo, Brazil, and was fortunate to have Yoshiharu Kohayakawa and Susanna de Rezende as supervisors.

I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science, probability theory, and algebra. My main interests are in computational complexity, specifically in proving lower bounds for computational models.
